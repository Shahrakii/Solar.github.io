Venus or Phosphorus It is the second planet closest to the Sun and is located between Earth and Mercury. On Venus, every 1 year is equivalent to 225 days on Earth. Venus is the closest planet to Earth, that's why it is called Earth's sister. Venus is the brightest planet that can be seen in the night sky. Venus has no moon and is the hottest planet in the solar system. The temperature of the planet is 481 °C is. Venus has active eruptions. Venus is slightly smaller compared to Earth and its gravity is very similar to that of Earth. Each day and night on Venus is equivalent to 243 days on Earth.
