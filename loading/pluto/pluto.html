Pluto is the dwarf planet of the Kuiper belt in the solar system and became the ninth planet in the solar system. The planet Pluto has a rock and ice structure and is a small sphere with a mass of one third of the moon. Pluto has 5 moons. Pluto's structure is mostly composed of frozen nitrogen, ice water, and rock. One year on Pluto lasts 248 Earth years.
