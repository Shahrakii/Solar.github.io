Mars or Bahram is the fourth planet in the solar system that revolves around the sun at a lower speed than the Earth. The length of the ecliptic on this planet is equal to 687 Earth days. Mars is smaller than the Earth and its diameter is 6790 km. Plants grow in a flower house on Mars. The atmosphere of Mars is red and can be seen in red color from Earth at night. Mars has 2 moons. Like Earth, Mars has polar ice, deep valleys, mountains, storms and seasons.
