Another name for Mercury is Hermes and it is the closest planet to the sun. This planet orbits the sun once every 88 days Mercury has no moon and the surface facing the sun is very hot and the side not facing the sun is cold. The temperature of this planet is -173°C at night and 427°C during the day. The intensity of the magnetic field of Mercury is one tenth of that of Earth. The distance between Mercury and Earth is 80 million kilometers. Mount Mercury does not have eruptions and mountains. The solar winds constantly blow to the surface of Mercury and therefore the air there has no atmosphere
