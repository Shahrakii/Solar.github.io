Uranus is the seventh planet in terms of proximity to the sun and the third planet in terms of size in the solar system. Uranus orbits the sun once every 84 years and 7 days. It is also equivalent to 17 hours and 14 minutes on Earth. Uranus has 23 moons. Unlike other planets, this planet does not have a significant internal heat source. This planet is seen in blue-green color.
