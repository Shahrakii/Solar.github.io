Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and is the fifth planet after Mars in terms of distance from the sun. The length of a day in Jupiter is equal to 9 hours and 56 minutes. The most obvious feature of Jupiter is the presence of a large red spot on its surface. The lowest air temperature on this planet is -145°C and the highest is 21°C. Jupiter has 92 moons, the largest of which is called Ganymede. Ganymede is larger than the planet Mercury. Jupiter has 3 narrow rings around it, which are made of tiny dust particles.
